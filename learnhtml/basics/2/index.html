The basics

The body:

After the head, the body follows:

End of previous code listing...
  <body>
    <h1>Hello, World!</h1>
    <h6>This is the smallest heading</h6>
    <p>Hi there!</p>
  </body>
</html>

Here is what everything means:

  • <body>: This tag indicates that the enclosed section should be displayed on the browser.
  • <h1>: This is the tag to indicate a level 1 heading (the largest).
  • <h6>: This is the level 6 heading (smallest).
  • <p>: This tag indicates a paragraph. Some elements can be nested in it (will be covered later).

These tags enable your browser to actually show your webpage. The heading tags can be anywhere from h1 to h6.
